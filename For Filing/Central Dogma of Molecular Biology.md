 The [[Central Dogma of Molecular Biology]] is a theory stating that genetic information flows only in one direction, from [[Deoxyribonucleic Acid|DNA]] to [[Ribonucleic Acids|RNA]], [[Proteins]], or [[Ribonucleic Acids|RNA]] directly to proteins.

$$\ce{ DNA -> RNA -> Proteins }$$
