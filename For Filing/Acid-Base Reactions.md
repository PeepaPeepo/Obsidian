[[Chemical Reactions]] between [[Acids]] and [[Bases]] are known as [[Acid-Base Reactions]], or [[Acid-Base Reactions|Neutralization Reactions]]. It's key feature is that it produces [[Ionic Compounds|Salts]] and $\ce{ H_{2}O }$.
