"Middle, Skin"

[[Mesoderm]] uniquely apply to [[Triploblasty]] [[Organisms]] and is formed within a [[Blastula]] undergoing [[Gastrulation]]. This layer is between the [[Ectoderm]] and the [[Endoderm]] and fills the space. The mesoderm develops into [[Cardiac Muscle Cells]], [[Skeletal Muscle Cells]], [[Tubule Cells]] of [[Kidneys]], [[Red Blood Cells]], [[Smooth Muscle Cells]] in gut.
