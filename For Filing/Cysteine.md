[[Cysteine]], abbreviated as Cys and C, is an [[Amino Acids]] with a [[Side Chains]] that is similar to [[Alanine]]'s, but with a [[Sulfhydryl Group]] attached to it.

Cysteine side chain = [[Alanine]] side chain + $\ce{ - SH }$
