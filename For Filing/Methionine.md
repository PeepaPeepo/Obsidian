[[Methionine]], abbreviated as Met or M, is an [[Amino Acids|amino acid]] where it's [[Side Chains|side chain]] is composed of modified three [[Methyl Group|methyl groups]] and a [[Sulfur]]. It is a [[Nonpolar]] side chain.
