- [[Side Chains]] when connected to [[Alpha Carbons]] that have an [[Amino Group]], a [[Carboxyl Groups]], and a [[Hydrogen]] forms the [[Monomers|monomer]], [[Amino Acids]]

Side chains include the following: 

# Nonpolar Side Chains

- [[Glycine]]
- [[Alanine]]
- [[Valine]]
- [[Leucine]]
- [[Isoleucine]]
- [[Methionine]]
- [[Phenylalanine]]
- [[Tryptophan]]
- [[Proline]]

# Polar Side Chains

- [[Serine]]
- [[Threonine]]
- [[Cysteine]]
- [[Tyrosine]]
- [[Asparagine]]
- [[Glutamine]]

# Ionized Side Chains

- [[Aspartic Acid]]
- [[Glutamic Acid]]
- [[Lysine]]
- [[Arginine]]
- [[Histidine]]
