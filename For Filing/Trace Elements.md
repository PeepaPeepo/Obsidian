- Trace elements are still part of the [[Essential Elements|essential elements]]

	- Copper
	- Fluorine
	- Iron
	- Manganese
	- Molybdenum
	- Selenium
	- [[Silicon]]]
	- [[Tin]]
	- [[Vanadium]]
